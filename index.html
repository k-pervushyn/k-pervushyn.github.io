Kyrylo Pervushyn's Personal Website

This website is meant for publishing my research ideas in form of articles. My areas of interest are Mathematics, especially Topology and Differential Geometry, some areas of Physics (Classical, Celestial and Quantum Mechanics), as well as programming languages and theory of compilation.

I am proficient with a bunch of programming languages, main ones being C/C++, Rust, Haskell. However, as a student in Computer Science, I've had lots of experience in Java and Python.
